FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

🔌 Easy Setup?

Power up, connect to Wi-Fi, and start streaming platforms like Netflix instantly. Connect gaming consoles with HDMI and adjust focus for a crystal-clear view.

📦 What’s Included?

Everything you need: the projector, adapter, remote, and manual.

🎥 Stream Netflix Directly?

Absolutely! Netflix is pre-installed on your VisionBeam™ for your convenience. Enjoy streaming your favorite shows and movies directly without the need for additional devices. Please note, while the VisionBeam™ allows you to access streaming platforms, subscriptions to services like Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney+, and others are not included and must be obtained separately.

🎞️ Compatibility with Streaming Platforms?

Access a wide range of platforms, including Netflix, YouTube, Amazon Prime, Disney+, Apple TV+ and more. Subscription fees for these services are not included with the purchase of VisionBeam™ and need to be acquired independently.

📱 Compatible with iPhone and Android?

Absolutely! VisionBeam™ is designed to work seamlessly with both iPhone and Android devices. Here’s how you can connect your smartphone for streaming or screen mirroring:


For iPhone and Mac Users:

We recommend using the Airscreen or Eshare apps, which are available on the Google Play Store on your VisionBeam™. These apps are specially designed to enhance your streaming experience. Simply follow the on-screen instructions provided by these apps to set up Airplay on your projector. For detailed guidance, check out the AirScreen tutorials available here.


For Android Users:

Android users can utilize the pre-installed Miracast app for easy screen mirroring. This built-in feature allows you to share content directly from your Android device to the VisionBeam™ without the need for additional hardware. This integration ensures that you can enjoy a variety of content from your mobile device on a larger screen, enhancing your viewing experience whether you're watching movies, sharing photos, or presenting documents. The setup is straightforward and designed to get you started quickly and effortlessly.

📲 Wireless Connection?

VisionBeam™'s Bluetooth capability allows for easy pairing with your devices.

🛠️ Need Extra Devices?

No need. Stream content and mirror screens without additional hardware.

Yes, VisionBeam™ proudly supports HDR (High Dynamic Range), enhancing your viewing experience with brighter whites, deeper blacks, and a wider color spectrum. Immerse yourself in vivid, lifelike images that bring your content to life, whether it’s movies, games, or photos.

🔊 Bluetooth Audio Compatibility?

Absolutely! VisionBeam™ seamlessly connects to your Bluetooth devices, including air buds and speakers, for an enhanced auditory experience. Enjoy clear, high-quality sound wirelessly, perfect for movies, music, or gaming sessions.
